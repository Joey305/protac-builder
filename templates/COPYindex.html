Welcome to the PROTAC Builder

Thank you for using our platform! This interactive builder allows rapid prototyping of PROTAC molecules by combining warheads, linkers, and E3 ligase recruiters.

You can modify individual pieces, load from templates, or explore ligase recruiters from the integrated Recruiter Ligandalyzer module.
